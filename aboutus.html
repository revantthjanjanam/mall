A Few Words About Time Squre Mall


The Times square a shopping mall located on Hosur Road in Koramangala, Bangalore, Karnataka, India developed by IIITDM Group. It is the largest shopping mall in the city of Bangalore. The Times square is the oldest full-fledged mall in Bangalore with a multi-story book store, 12 cinema halls and other entertainment facilities. The mall is an attraction for tourists. The shopping mall houses 72,000 m2 (780,000 sq ft) of shops over five levels .
